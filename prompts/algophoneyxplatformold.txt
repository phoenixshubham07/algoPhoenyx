# AlgoPhoenyx
## AI-Powered Learning Assistant for DSA, System Design, and Development Progress

An intelligent platform that tracks your Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA), System Design, and development progress while fetching real-time questions from the web. The system teaches you simultaneously and builds a custom question library tailored to your level, similar to Duolingo but for technical interview preparation.

**Platform Tagline**: *Rise, Learn, Excel - Your AI-Powered Interview Success Platform*
